Fun dice game - The first player to score 100 wins the game. It requires strategy and luck to win the game.
This is a two-player game. A fun way for kids to learn strategies (to hold by taking their chances)
Rules for the game: - Each turn, a player rolls the dice and accumulates points. But don't get greedy. - If Player rolls ONE, the player gives up the turn and all current chance points will be lost. - If a player taps on HOLD, all points of the current turn will be added to the main score. - First player to reach 100 points wins the game!
Scoring Example: Scenario 1: Player1 rolls a 3 and decides to continue. Player1 again tolls 3 more times (5,3,1). Because Player1 rolled 1, so all scores will be lost and players earn 0 points. Now turn goes to Player2. Scenario 2: Player2 rolls a 6 and rolls 3 more times (4,2,5) and decide to click on HOLD. So the total of 6+4+2+5=17, 17 will get added to the main score, and turn goes to Player1. Scenario 3: When a player reaches a total of 100 or more points, the game ends and that player is the winner.
